Our Story

We at Ration have identified a problem with food waste, specifically, household food waste. It is a common experience to go grocery shopping, and to end up discarding half of the food purchased by the end of the week. This is a common pattern across the country, and about 80 million pounds of food is wasted each year.

Because of this, we decided to create a website that guides users in food waste prevention. There are similar apps on the market like TooGoodToGo. However, TooGoodToGo focuses on restaurant food waste, not household food waste.
